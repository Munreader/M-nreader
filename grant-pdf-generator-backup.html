Executive Summary

Münreader is one of the world's first dual e‑ink sanctuary devices — a distraction‑free artifact designed to restore focus and presence in age of noise. Encased in vegan suede with engraved permanence, Münreader merges cutting‑edge hardware with cultural intention: a device that wants nothing from its user.

Its dual‑screen architecture separates ambient display ("Explore") from active reading, creating a new genre of reading technology. With microSD sovereignty, a sleep headphone jack, and a minimalistic UI free from notifications, Münreader is not a productivity tool — it is sanctuary hardware born in Canada.

This grant will support prototype development, compliance testing (FCC, CE, RoHS), and supply chain sovereignty for first 100 pilot units. With your partnership, Canada can position itself as the birthplace of sanctuary hardware — a cultural export that redefines reading technology.

Founder's Why

Münreader began as a personal necessity. As a lifelong reader, researcher, and ritual‑driven creator, I needed a device that honoured the depth of reading without the noise of modern technology. No existing tool protected my focus, my data, or my creative rituals. So I built the device I could not find — a sanctuary in hardware form. Münreader exists because deep reading deserves a vessel worthy of it.

Innovation

Münreader introduces a dual e‑ink configuration that enables simultaneous ambient presence and deep reading. This architecture is paired with sovereign design principles:

  • Dual e‑ink screens for ambient + active reading
  • Minimalistic UI with no notifications
  • MicroSD expansion for data sovereignty
  • Sleep headphone jack for ritual reading
  • Ambient spine glow for low‑light sanctuary
  • Vegan suede cover with engraved permanence

This configuration eliminates the need for multi‑device workflows and restores the cognitive continuity lost in modern reading environments.
