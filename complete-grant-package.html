Executive Summary

Münreader is one of the world's first dual e‑ink sanctuary devices — a distraction‑free artifact designed to restore focus and presence in the age of noise. Encased in vegan suede with engraved permanence, Münreader merges cutting‑edge hardware with cultural intention: a device that wants nothing from its user.

Its dual‑screen architecture separates ambient display ("Explore") from active reading, creating a new genre of reading technology. With microSD sovereignty, a sleep headphone jack, and a minimalistic UI free from notifications, Münreader is not a productivity tool — it is sanctuary hardware born in Canada.

This grant will support prototype development, compliance testing (FCC, CE, RoHS), and supply chain sovereignty for the first 100 pilot units. With your partnership, Canada can position itself as the birthplace of sanctuary hardware — a cultural export that redefines reading technology.
